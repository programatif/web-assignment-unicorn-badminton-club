Our Club

We really welcome visitors so please do come and try us out - the club is a great night out whether you are an experienced player, someone who played a long while back and is now interested in returning to badminton, or someone wanting to progress your play on from casual to a bit more competitive.

We particularly welcome younger players (juniors and students) to help you enjoy and develop your game, and to gain an introduction to a lifetime of healthy, enjoyable sport.
